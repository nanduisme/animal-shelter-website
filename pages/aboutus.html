About A.A.C.I

A.A.C.I (Animal Adoption and Care Interface) is a non-profit organization dedicated to finding loving homes for abandoned and homeless pets. Our mission is to provide a platform where people can easily connect with furry companions in need of a forever family.

At A.A.C.I, we believe that every pet deserves a second chance at a happy life. We work tirelessly to rescue, rehabilitate, and rehome animals of all kinds, from dogs and cats to rabbits, birds, and more. Our team of dedicated volunteers ensures that each animal receives proper care, love, and attention until they find their perfect match.

We are committed to promoting responsible pet ownership and educating the community on the importance of animal welfare. Join us in our mission to create a world where no pet is left behind.
